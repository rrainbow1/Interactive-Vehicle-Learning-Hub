Toe — viewed from above

Toe is the direction the wheels point relative to the vehicle centreline when viewed from above. Front edges closer together = toe-in; further apart = toe-out.

Camber — viewed from the front

Camber is wheel tilt viewed from the front. Top of wheel inward = negative camber; top outward = positive camber.

Caster — viewed from the side

Caster is the steering-axis tilt viewed from the side. Positive caster means the upper steering-axis point is behind the lower point.

Use the sliders to change alignment angles and watch the wheel positions move in the correct viewing direction.
