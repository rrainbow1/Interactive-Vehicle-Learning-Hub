Teaching model: This simulator represents a conventional brushed DC low-pressure fuel pump. The yellow trace is supply voltage and the cyan trace is current measured with a low-amp clamp. The current ripple represents commutator segments. Exact current, speed and voltage vary by vehicle, fuel pressure, pump type and operating conditions.
